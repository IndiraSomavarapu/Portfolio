bio

I am Indira Somavarapu, a working professional living in Baton Rouge, LA with my family. I was born and raised in a village called Edlapalli, Andhra Pradesh Southern State of India. Then, I moved to Bangalore after getting married and started as an Accountant, then as a Receptionist, and finally moved into pursuing my career in IT. While living in India, I worked for fortune 500 Tech companies and eventually moved to the USA in 2006 for better career opportunities.

After moving to States, worked in the Northeast region for a couple of years and finally moved to Louisiana in 2010. Since then I have been working with H&E at Baton Rouge as SAP Technical Lead (11 years). I had an amazing journey in my professional career which has taught me to believe in hard work, dedication, and commitment. I am hoping BU's MSSD program helps in achieving further professional growth and leads to more career opportunities.
